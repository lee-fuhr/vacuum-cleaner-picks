Best for specific needs

Best for pet owners

If you share your home with shedding dogs or cats, the Dyson Ball Animal 3’s self-cleaning brush roll and superior suction make it the top pick. It actively removes hair from the brush as you clean, so you won’t have to stop and cut tangled strands. The whole-machine HEPA filtration also helps trap dander, keeping the air cleaner. Our pick: Dyson Ball Animal 3 Upright Vacuum.

Best for tight budgets

The Hoover WindTunnel 3 delivers solid carpet cleaning for a fraction of the cost of premium models. It’s light, simple to operate, and handles everyday dirt and debris well. If you need to save money but can’t sacrifice core performance, this is the best value upright for 2026. Our pick: Hoover WindTunnel 3 Upright Vacuum UH30300.

Best for homes with allergies

The Kenmore Elite 31140 uses a sealed HEPA system and a large bag that traps dust and pollen without releasing it back into the room. Its automatic height adjustment and strong suction make it effective on carpets, and the bag holds more dirt so you empty it less often. For allergy sufferers who prefer a bagged vacuum, this is a solid choice. Our pick: Kenmore Elite 31140 Upright Vacuum.

Our verdict

For most people, the Shark Navigator Lift-Away NV350 remains the upright to beat, it’s versatile, affordable, and cleans well on all surfaces. If you can stretch your budget and live with pets, the Dyson Ball Animal 3 is worth the extra cost for its self-cleaning brush roll and raw power. Budget buyers will be happy with the Hoover WindTunnel 3, and those who prefer a bagged system should consider the Kenmore Elite. Whichever you choose, any of these five will keep your floors looking clean in 2026.
